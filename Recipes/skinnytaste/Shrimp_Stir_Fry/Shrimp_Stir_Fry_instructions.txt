1. To make the sauce, whisk the water and cornstarch in a small bowl until smooth. Add the soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, ginger, and sriracha (if using). Set aside.
2. Pour in the sauce, stir to coat, and cook until the sauce is glossy and starting to thicken, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
3. Serve with cooked rice and sprinkle with sesame seeds just before serving.
