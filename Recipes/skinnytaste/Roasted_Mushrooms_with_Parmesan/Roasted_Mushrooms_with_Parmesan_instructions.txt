1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  Spray 2 sheet pans with olive oil.
2. Divide mushrooms evenly among the 2 prepared sheet pans.
3. Drizzle each with 1 tablespoon olive oil, ¼ teaspoon kosher salt, pepper and half the fresh thyme then toss to evenly coat.  Spread the mushrooms out in an even layer.
4. Roast mushrooms for 20 minutes, mixing then rotating pans halfway through cooking.
5. Top with Parmesan, a drizzle of balsamic vinegar and garnish with more fresh thyme.
