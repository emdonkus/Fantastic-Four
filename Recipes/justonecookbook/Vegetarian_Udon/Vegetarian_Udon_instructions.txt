1. Gather all the ingredients.
2. To Make Vegan Dashi Broth2½ cups water, 1 piece kombu, and 2 pieces dried shiitake mushrooms. Steep for at least 30 minutes or overnight.
3. To Make the Crispy Seasoned Eggplant1 Japanese or Chinese eggplant and cut into roughly 2-inch wedges with the skin-on. The skin will hold the shape together. Without the skin, the flesh will become too soft.1 Tbsp potato starch or cornstarch and coat the eggplant nicely.3–4 Tbsp oil is not enough, then add more. Make sure to adjust the heat so it doesn’t take a long time to brown the eggplant. You don’t want overcooked and mushy eggplant. 1 Tbsp mirin and 1 Tbsp soy sauce.
4. To Prepare the Other Toppings3 oz deep-fried firm tofu cutlet (atsuage) into thin slices.2 Shanghai bok choy in half lengthwise.
5. To Make the Sweet and Savory Minced Mushrooms2 shiitake mushrooms and slice the caps thinly.
6. Cut the mushroom caps into thin slices and then mince them.½ Tbsp sugar, and 1 Tbsp soy sauce.2–3 Tbsp water and bring it to a simmer. Continue adding water so the bottom of the pot has some liquid.
7. To Make the Udon Soup Broth1 Tbsp mirin, 1 tsp sugar, and 1 Tbsp soy sauce.¼ tsp Diamond Crystal kosher salt and the fried tofu. Turn off the heat and cover (so it won’t evaporate).
8. To Blanch the Bok Choy, Cook the Noodles, and Assemble12 oz udon noodles according to the package instructions.shichimi togarashi (Japanese seven spice) on the side for a spicy kick.
