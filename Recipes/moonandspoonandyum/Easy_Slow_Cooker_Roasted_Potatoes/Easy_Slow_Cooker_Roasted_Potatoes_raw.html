Slow Cooker Roasted Potatoes Recipe

Why You Will Love This Recipe

  • It really doesn’t get much easier than this when it comes to a perfect side dish!
  • You can enjoy these Crockpot potatoes as is or customize them to suit your tastes.
  • They make a great everyday side dish or an excellent side for holidays like Thanksgiving or Christmas.
  • The flavor is really satisfying.
  • Crock pot potatoes are naturally vegan, dairy-free, gluten-free, soy-free, and nut-free.

What You Will Need To Make This Recipe

To make slow cooker roast potatoes, you will need a slow cooker like a Crock-Pot, or an electric pressure cooker with a slow cooking function.

Ingredients and Substitutes

Russet potatoes: I think Russet potatoes are the ultimate slow-cooking potato, but you can replace them with red potatoes, gold potatoes, fingerling potatoes, or any potato you prefer with similar results

Spices: I really love the combo of paprika, garlic, and oregano, but if you aren’t a fan, feel free to omit or replace any of them.

Oil: Olive oil and avocado oil taste best with these potatoes, but feel free to use what you have on hand.

How-to Make

Serving Ideas

  • Top with fresh herbs like rosemary, dill, thyme, or basil before serving.
  • Sprinkle with shredded cheese for a few minutes before turning off the slow cooker.
  • For a bit of indulgence, top with a pat of butter a few minutes before turning off the slow cooker.
  • Top with freshly chopped chives or green onions before serving.
  • Serve with sour cream, guacamole, or salsa.
  • Serve with a good hot sauce.
  • Serve with a dipping sauce like garlic aioli sauce or romesco sauce.
  • Serve alongside main courses like vegan meatloaf, air fryer falafel, or stuffed acorn squash.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do potatoes go mushy in the slow cooker?

No. Potatoes maintain their structure rather well in a slow cooker.

Can slow cooker potatoes be stored?

Yes. Slow-cooked potatoes make excellent leftovers and can be stored in the refrigerator for up to one week and reheated in a slow cooker or on the stovetop until warm.

Slow Cooker Roasted Potatoes Recipe

These slow cooker roasted potatoes make for one super easy Crockpot recipe! The perfect potato side dish is within reach!

Ingredients

  • 5 russet potatoes cubed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or avocado oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
Prevent your screen from going dark

Instructions

  • Place the potatoes, oil, garlic powder, paprika, oregano and salt into the slow cooker. Toss to coat.
  • Slow cook on low for 4 hours or on high for 2 hours, or until the potatoes are tender and golden.
  • Enjoy!

Notes

As served in the photos: I like to sprinkle some parmesan cheese and everything seasoning on the potatoes a few minutes before serving.
Cook time: My slow cooker takes 2 hours on high and 4 on low, but depending on the slow cooker used and how thickly the potatoes are cut it could take as long as 3 on high or 5 on low.
The skin: I prefer to keep the skin on, but if you aren’t a fan, feel free to peel the potatoes instead.
