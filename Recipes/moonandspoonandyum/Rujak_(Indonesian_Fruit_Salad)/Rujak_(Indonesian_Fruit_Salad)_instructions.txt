1. In a large bowl, add the mango, grapefruit, orange, cucumber and papaya. Set aside.
2. In a small saucepan over high heat, add the water, sugar, chiles, nuts, and salt.
3. Bring all to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Simmer for 10 minutes stirring frequently, until all has reduced and is syrup-like in consistency.
4. Pour the dressing over the fruit, then toss all to combine.
5. Serve and enjoy!
